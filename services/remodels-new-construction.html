Remodels & New Construction Electrical

Expert wiring for builds and renovations throughout Utah

Building or Remodeling? We're Your Electrical Partner

From new custom homes to kitchen remodels and basement finishes, Osborne Electric & Contracting provides comprehensive electrical services for construction projects of all sizes.

We work directly with homeowners, builders, and general contractors to deliver quality electrical work that meets all codes and exceeds expectations.

  • New home construction wiring
  • Basement finish electrical
  • Home additions and expansions
  • Kitchen and bathroom remodels
  • Rough-in and finish electrical
  • Permit coordination and inspections
  • Code-compliant installations
  • Builder partnerships

Our Construction Process

We understand that timing is critical on construction projects. That's why we coordinate closely with your project timeline to ensure electrical work never holds up progress.

What We Handle:

  • Initial consultation and electrical planning
  • Load calculations and panel sizing
  • Permit applications and coordination
  • Rough-in wiring during framing phase
  • Trim-out and device installation
  • Final testing and inspection

Project Types We Handle

Custom Home Builds

Complete electrical systems for new custom homes, from planning to final walkthrough. We work with your builder or directly with you.
