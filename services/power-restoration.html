Common Causes of Power Loss

Understanding why you lost power helps us fix it faster

Tripped Breaker

Overloaded circuits or short circuits cause breakers to trip. If resetting doesn't work, there's an underlying issue we need to diagnose.

GFCI Trip

GFCI outlets protect against ground faults. When they trip, outlets downstream also lose power. We'll find the root cause.

Loose Connections

Connections loosen over time, especially in older homes. Loose wires cause intermittent power loss and fire hazards.

Damaged Wiring

Rodents, age, and physical damage can compromise wiring. We locate and repair damaged sections safely.

Panel Issues

Failing breakers, bus bar problems, or panel damage can cause power loss. We diagnose and repair panel issues.
