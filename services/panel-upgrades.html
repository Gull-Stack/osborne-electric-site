Electrical Panel Upgrades

Safe, modern panels for today's power demands

Is Your Electrical Panel Keeping Up?

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's electrical system. Older panels can struggle to meet modern power demands, leading to tripped breakers, safety hazards, and limitations on what you can add to your home.

Osborne Electric specializes in panel upgrades for Utah homes. Whether you need to replace an outdated panel or upgrade to 200 amp service for an EV charger, we've got you covered.

Signs You Need a Panel Upgrade:

  • Frequent breaker trips
  • Burning smell from the panel
  • Rust, corrosion, or visible damage
  • 100 amp service (or less)
  • Federal Pacific or Zinsco panel
  • Adding major appliances or EV charging
  • Home is over 25 years old
  • Fuse box instead of breakers

Our Panel Upgrade Services

We handle all aspects of electrical panel work, from simple breaker replacements to complete service upgrades.
