Popular Lighting Upgrades

The most requested lighting installations from our Utah customers

Recessed Lighting

Clean, modern recessed lights that provide even illumination without taking up visual space. Perfect for living rooms, kitchens, and basements.

Kitchen Lighting

Layered kitchen lighting including recessed fixtures, under-cabinet task lighting, and pendant lights over islands. Make your kitchen shine.

Outdoor Lighting

Enhance curb appeal and security with professional outdoor lighting. Porch lights, path lights, deck lighting, and landscape illumination.

LED Conversions

Upgrade your existing fixtures to energy-efficient LED bulbs or fixtures. Lower your electric bills and get better light quality.

Smart Lighting

Control your lights from your phone, set schedules, and integrate with smart home systems like Alexa, Google Home, or Apple HomeKit.

Dimmer Installation

Add dimmer switches to control light levels and set the perfect mood. We install traditional and smart dimmers compatible with your fixtures.
